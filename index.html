Our Service

Aimed at providing high-end, convenient snack and refreshment solutions, our vending machines will be strategically located at the main level near the trolley carts, ensuring easy access for all residents, guests, and staff. Offering a curated selection of gourmet snacks and drinks, we address the need for quality and convenience. Our commitment to maintaining and regularly servicing the machines guarantees a seamless, 24/7 snacking experience. With Concierge Vending Co., residents will enjoy an upscale, reliable source of refreshments right within their building.

Who We Serve

Our primary customers are the residents, guests, and staff of Cora Apartments who seek quick, convenient access to quality snacks and beverages. We recognize the diverse demographic of the community and aim to cater to their varied tastes and preferences.

What We Offer

We will offer a carefully selected range of products, including healthy snack options, gourmet treats, artisanal beverages, and popular branded items. Our selection process emphasizes quality, freshness, and variety to ensure we meet the high standards expected by our customers. Concierge Vending Co. is open to taking suggestions from the Cora residents to better accommodate their needs.

How We Operate

Our vending machines will be placed on the main level near the trolley carts. This area has been chosen for its ease of access and visibility. Maintenance and restocking will be conducted as needed based on the machine’s remote inventory management system to ensure consistent service quality.

Sustainability Matters

Understanding the importance of Water Street’s environmental commitment as a first WELL certified community, our business will prioritize eco-friendly practices, including Energy Star Rated machines and biodegradable packaging where possible.

Our Machines

Concierge Vending Co. proudly introduces an innovative vending machine, combining sleek design and substantial capacity, capable of offering 217 high-end snacks and 136 premium beverages. This machine features tap-to-pay technology, as well as traditional cash, ensuring ease and security in transactions. Equipped with a sophisticated inventory management system, it provides real-time data on stock levels, allowing for consistent and convenient restocking. Notably, the machine is Energy Star rated, exemplifying our commitment to sustainability, ensuring energy efficiency without compromising on functionality and user experience.
